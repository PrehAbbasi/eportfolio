Initial discussion on the opportunities and limitations of large language models (LLMs)

Critically evaluated LLMs as assistive rather than autonomous tools, recognising hallucinations, bias and accountability challenges. This strengthened my understanding of ANN applications, ethical AI deployment and the importance of professional judgement when evaluating AI generated outputs within real world decision making contexts.
