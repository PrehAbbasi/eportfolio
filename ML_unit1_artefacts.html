Unit 1 Artefacts

Initial discussion

For the Unit 1 discussion, I analysed the Maersk NotPetya cyberattack to examine how increasing digitalisation and interconnected systems create both opportunities and challenges within Industry 4.0. The discussion demonstrated that while data-driven technologies improve operational efficiency, they also introduce cybersecurity risks that can disrupt intelligent systems. This activity strengthened my understanding of the wider context in which machine learning operates and highlighted the importance of balancing technological innovation with organisational resilience and responsible digital transformation (Zhong et al., 2017; Metcalf, 2024).

References

Zhong, R.Y., Xu, C., Klotz, E. and Newman, S.T. (2017) ‘Intelligent manufacturing in the context of Industry 4.0: A review’, International Journal of Production Research, 56(1–2), pp. 508–534
